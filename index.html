About the Artist

Suresh Moorthy writes orchestral music that treats Indian and Western classical traditions as equals, not opposites. Inspired by Vangelis, his sound is symphonic and electronic at once, built from melody outward rather than from chords.

While his debut album, A Daylight Promise: A Symphonic Suite, arrives this fall, its closing movement, Earth in Heaven's Embrace, has already won the One Earth Music Award.

All compositions, arrangements, and production by Suresh Moorthy.
