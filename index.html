Winner: One Earth Music Award (Season 11)

Suresh Moorthy

A Daylight Promise: A Symphonic Suite

Suresh Moorthy is an award winning composer whose music blends Eastern and Western traditions through a symphonic, cinematic approach. Drawing inspiration from the electronic legacy of Vangelis, he combines orchestral arrangements with New Age textures to create immersive musical landscapes. His upcoming project, A Daylight Promise: A Symphonic Suite, highlights a unique fusion of orchestral depth and classical Indian melodic structures.

A Daylight Promise: A Symphonic Suite

First Movement Breath Amid the Dust

Strings and woodwinds in sonata form, tracing the emergence of life in the world of matter.

Second Movement Before the Candle Falls

Strings, piano and timpani set in India’s tala system, capturing innocence before it grows into experience.

Third Movement Knowledge, Half-Bitten

A polyphony for strings, piano and woodwinds, showing how knowledge can obscure our true nature.

Fourth Movement Between Reality and If Only

A counterpoint for strings, where today meets yesterday’s folly.

Fifth Movement Ransom in a Raindrop

Strings sustained over a Carnatic inspired drone, illuminating the path forward amid mist and missteps.

Sixth Movement (Award Winner) Earth in Heaven’s Embrace

Strings and timpani, shaped by the raga system of India, foretelling the meeting of the high and the low.
